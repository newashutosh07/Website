Attack On Titan

"Attack on Titan" is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Hajime Isayama. It is set in a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures called Titans. These Titans devour humans seemingly without reason. The story revolves around Eren Yeager and his friends Mikasa and Armin, who join the military to fight against the Titans after their hometown is destroyed. As they uncover the mysteries of the Titans and the truth about their world, they face both external and internal threats, leading to intense battles and emotional struggles. The series explores themes of survival, humanity, and the consequences of war.
